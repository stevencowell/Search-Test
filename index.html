Welcome to the Stage 6 Construction Hub

This page is your central access point for all supplementary learning materials for the Stage 6 Construction VET course. Each of the seven main assessment tasks is listed below. Clicking on a task will take you to a dedicated page that provides a summary of the assessment requirements and links to detailed modules. These modules contain the essential theory, notes, and information to help you master the concepts and successfully complete your assessments.

From this page, you can navigate to resources for:

  • Task 1: White Card: Fundamental WHS for the construction industry.
  • Task 2: Tools & Equipment: Safe use and maintenance of hand and power tools.
  • Task 3: Work Safe: Applying WHS policies and managing hazards.
  • Task 4: Working It Out: Construction calculations and basic project costing.
  • Task 5.3: Joinery: Manufacturing and assembling joinery components.
  • Task 6: Project Planning: Reading plans and organising work tasks.
  • Task 7: Group Project: A culminating team-based construction project.
